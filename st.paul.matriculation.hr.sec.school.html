About St. Paul Matric School

St. Paul Matric School in Mangalampettai is dedicated to nurturing young minds through a balanced blend of academic excellence and moral education. Our experienced teachers ensure each child receives personalised attention and opportunities to explore creativity.

Child-Centered Learning

Activity-based lessons, safe classrooms and caring mentors.

Strong Values

We encourage respect, responsibility, and teamwork.

Community

Regular parent-teacher meets and local community programs.

Academics & Learning

Our curriculum follows the Matriculation board and is enhanced with practical labs, projects, quizzes, and periodic assessments. We focus on both scholastic skills and co-curricular development.

Classrooms

Smart boards, neat seating, and activity corners.

Science Labs

Well-equipped labs for hands-on experiments and safety training.

Computer Lab

Practical coding, digital literacy and supervised access.

Library

A cozy library with age-wise reading sections and study spaces.

Programs & Activities

We believe learning goes beyond books. Our programs include:

  • • Sports & physical education (annual sports day, coaching)
  • • Cultural activities (dance, music, drama)
  • • Science fairs & project exhibitions
  • • Clubs: Eco-club, Maths club, Art club

Facilities & Campus

The campus is designed with safety and learning in mind: clean drinking water, secure perimeter, CCTV monitored areas, dedicated medical room, and hygienic washrooms. Outdoor sports ground and an assembly stage for cultural events.

Safe Campus

Secure gates, first-aid, and staff supervision.

Transport

School buses with trained drivers and safety protocols.

Canteen

Hygienic food options and periodic audits.

FAQ — Common Questions

What are the school timings?⯈
School runs from 8:30 AM to 3:00 PM (Monday to Friday). Separate timings for pre-primary are followed.
How to apply for admission?⯈
Visit the school office or call the admissions number. Fill the application form and submit required documents.
Is transport available?⯈
Yes, we provide bus transport with safe routes and trained staff.
